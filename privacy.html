Privacy Policy — Aura Volleyball Scorekeeper

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Aura Volleyball Scorekeeper ("the app") is developed by Eric Fortuna.

The short version

The app collects no data. Period.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not collect, transmit, store remotely, or share any personal information or usage data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party services.

Data stored on your device

Match history (team names, set scores, dates) that you choose to save is stored only on your device. Deleting a match in the app, clearing history, or deleting the app removes this data permanently. It is never uploaded anywhere.

Sharing

When you use the Share feature, the app hands a plain-text match summary to the standard iOS share sheet. Where it goes from there (Messages, Mail, etc.) is controlled entirely by you and the app you share to.

Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.
