Quick tips

Tap anywhere on a team's side to add a point. Swipe down on a side to take a point back. Tap a team name to edit it, and use the color dot to change each side's neon color. Sets follow standard volleyball rules — to 25, deciding set to 15, win by 2. Completed matches can be saved and reviewed in Match History.
